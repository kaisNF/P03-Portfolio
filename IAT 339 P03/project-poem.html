Pre Production

As a director, I have met with my team multiple of times on a weekly basis to go over the vision for our poem video. I had an overview of the shots we are planned to take and helped assembling the shot list.

What I learned in the pre production phase is how to handle change in a rapid manner. Also, to always have different plans for shoots in case complications arise.

2.

Post Production: Editing

After shooting the footage needed I have taken over the editing phase of the project, I have color graded, stabilized, and assembled the footage to have a continuous sequence of our shots.

3.

Post Production: Sound Design

After finishing the editing phase of the project I started the sound design process; Foley sounds, Mixing and mastering of the music and sound effects

Reflection

Being a director behind a poem video does come with its challanges. As I had to follow an artistic style of composition rather than a linear and continous one like the narrative films, but with the help of my team's collaboration we figured out how to over come our challanages

An issue we came across mutiple of time was how to make this peotic video inresting and at the same time educational
